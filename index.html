Referral Program FAQ

How does the referral program work?

When you refer a friend to RedGirraffe, they'll receive a unique link. Once they sign up and make their first payment through our platform, you'll both receive 500 in RedGirraffe Cash Points.

Where can I see my referral earnings?

Your referral earnings are automatically added to your RedGirraffe Cash Points balance, which you can view in the Rewards section of your account.

Is there a limit to how many friends I can refer?

No! You can refer as many friends as you'd like. There's no cap on the number of referrals or the amount of RedGirraffe Cash Points you can earn through referrals.

How long does it take to receive my referral bonus?

Your referral bonus is credited immediately after your friend completes their first payment. You'll receive a notification confirming the bonus has been added to your account.

How do I add a new registration?

Click on the "Add New Registration" button in the Registrations section and fill out the required information.

How do I redeem RedPoints?

Visit the Rewards section and browse available rewards. Click "Redeem Now" on any reward you can afford.

How do I update my payment method?

Go to the Edit Records section, select your registration, and update the payment method details.
